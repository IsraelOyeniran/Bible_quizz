The New Testament

The New Testament serves as the foundation of Christian theology and practice, containing the teachings of Jesus and the early Christian leaders. It is considered by Christians as a guide for faith and practice, providing spiritual insight, moral guidance, and the promise of salvation through faith in Jesus Christ.

The Old Testament

The Old Testament serves as the sacred scripture for Judaism, laying the foundation for the Jewish faith. For Christians, it is the precursor to the New Testament, providing the historical and theological background for the life, teachings, and mission of Jesus Christ.
