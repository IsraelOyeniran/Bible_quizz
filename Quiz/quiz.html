    • The New Testament

    The New Testament serves as the foundation of Christian theology and practice, containing the teachings of Jesus and the early Christian leaders.     The Old Testament

    The Old Testament serves as the sacred scripture for Judaism, laying the foundation for the Jewish faith. For Christians, it is the precursor to the New Testament, providing the historical and theological background for the life, teachings, and mission of Jesus Christ.
